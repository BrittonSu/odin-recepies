About the dish

Pizza is one of the most popular and iconic dishes in our modern world. With the convience of various delivery services increasing, the need to bake your own pie is steadily declining. However, nothing can beat the taste and the fun of baking your own pizza pie.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Sauce
  • Pepperoni
  • Cheese
  1. Lay out bread
  2. Apply sauce evenly
  3. Apply cheese, make sure to cover the entire pie
  4. Add Pepperonis
  5. Finally, bake it in the oven.
